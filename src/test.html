Good Day,

Thank you once again for your interest in the Canadian Armed Forces.

To proceed with your application, please use the link below to schedule your appointments for the following steps:
    1.    Documentation Intake: If you have pending documents (official transcripts, foundational and supporting identification) to submit, please select this step. Ensure to bring the following:
    •    Foundational Identification:
    •    Canadian birth certificate
    •    Canadian citizenship certificate or citizenship card
    •    Permanent residence card
    •    Supporting Identification:
    •    Canadian passport
    •    Driver’s licence or enhanced driver’s licence
    •    Provincial photo card for non-drivers (excluding health cards)
    •    Secure Certificate of Indian Status issued by Crown-Indigenous Relations and Northern Affairs Canada
    •    Nexus card
    •    Firearms licence
    2.    CFAT & FORCE Testing: If you need to take or re-take the CFAT/FORCE test, choose this step.
    3.    Medical Appointment: If you still require a medical evaluation, select this step.

If you have any questions or need to bring additional items, you may also book a consultation appointment.

Important Notices:
    •    Parental/Guardian Attendance: If you are 16 or 17 years old, you must attend your first appointment with a parent or guardian.
    •    Communication Requirement: Failure to communicate with recruiting staff regarding emails or cancellations will negatively impact your file. If we do not hear from you within 60 days, your file will be closed.
    •    Attachments: Please review the attached files, as they may contain answers to your questions.
